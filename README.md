Privacy Policy – HealthBar IRL / QuestChat.app
Effective date: December 5, 2025
HealthBar IRL is a wellness and focus app created by Anthony Gagliardo (“we”, “us”, or “our”).
Information we collect
HealthBar IRL does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.
All data you enter in the app (such as focus sessions, quests, hydration, sleep, mood, or gut status) is stored locally on your device using Apple’s system frameworks. We do not operate our own backend or analytics service for this app.
Third-party services
HealthBar IRL does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social login providers.
Your data may be processed by Apple according to the terms of your device and App Store settings (such as iCloud backups, system diagnostics, or App Store analytics), which are governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.
Children
HealthBar IRL is intended for general use and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Changes to this policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Effective date” at the top of this page.
